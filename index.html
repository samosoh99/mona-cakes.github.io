History

Mona Cakes has been serving traditional European cakes in Ipoh, Perak since 2003.

Founded by a Spanish émigré, the bakery is known for its classic continental recipes, warm service, and old-world charm.

A local favourite for coffee and cake, Mona Cakes is where tradition meets indulgence. Every cake is baked with heritage, passion, and heart.

Since its founding, Mona Cakes has developed further and now provides a large selection of cakes for celebrations, birthdays, weddings, and catered events, all of which are made with the same commitment to quality and tradition.

Most of Mona recipes come from Eastern Europe, which includes Austria, Poland, Germany, Hungary, and Slovakia. Made the same way since 2003, the famous Polish cheesecake in Mona was even named one of the top four things to try in Ipoh by a leading Asian travel guide.
